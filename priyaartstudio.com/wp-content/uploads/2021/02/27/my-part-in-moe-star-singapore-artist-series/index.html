My part in MOE STAR Singapore Artist Series!

  • By: sathyapriya
  • Category: My Sketching journeyTagged: art interview, Gouache, landscapes, Light, MOE, Paintings, sketch2020, urbansketch, USKSG
  • February 27, 2021

MOE STAR video for Art Teachers n students is out! Patrick Ng , Parka Teoh Yi Chie and myself are featured and We hope to inspire teachers & students with our little snippets of practical approach to Art, thanks to Urban Sketchers Singapore for the wonderful opportunity!! Thank you Lorraine from MOE STAR and Onedash22 team!

We had so much fun giving the interviews and the shooting day was very exciting with our make-ups, multiple takes on the interviews & sketching Tiong Bahru shop houses!
